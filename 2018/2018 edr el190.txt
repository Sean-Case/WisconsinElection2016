Note regarding the EL-190NF: Election Voting and Registration Statistics Report
As of May 7, 2019, 6 of Wisconsin's applicable municipalities had not completed their statutorily-required reports:

Kenosha County - Village of Salem Lakes; Menominee County - Town of Menominee; Milwaukee County - Village of Brown Deer; Oconto County - Town of Abrams; Trempealeau County - Village of Ettrick and City of Galesville
